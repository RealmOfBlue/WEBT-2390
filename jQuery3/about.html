FAMILY-OWNED FUN

ABOUT THE OWNERS

Welcome to Billy Mac's Burgers and Rings, a labor of love crafted by the MacIntyre family. With a passion for delicious food and a dedication to creating memorable experiences, the MacIntyres have infused every aspect of our restaurant with warmth and charm. As a family-owned restaurant chain, we take pride in creating a welcoming and family-friendly atmosphere that keeps our guests coming back for more.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our delicious burgers and crispy onion rings to our state-of-the-art arcade, featuring a blend of classic game styles and cutting-edge VR technology. Step into our arcade and immerse yourself in the vibrant sights and sounds of the 80s, where every game session is a journey through retro gaming history. At Billy Macs, we're not just about great food; we're about creating lasting memories and bringing families together for a taste of old-school fun in a modern setting.

HOW WE STARTED

Billy Macs Burgers and Rings began as a dream shared by the MacIntyre family. It all started with Billy MacIntyre's passion for crafting the perfect burger recipe, inspired by his love for 80s arcade culture. With the support of his wife, Sarah, and their children, the idea of a family-owned restaurant blossomed into reality. Starting from humble beginnings in a small kitchen, Billy perfected his burger creations, infusing each patty with a blend of flavors that soon became the talk of the town. As word spread about the mouthwatering burgers and the lively arcade atmosphere, Billy Macs Burgers and Rings quickly grew into the beloved establishment it is today.

Our journey from a family's culinary vision to a thriving restaurant chain is a testament to our dedication to quality, creativity, and the joy of bringing people together over delicious food and nostalgic fun.
